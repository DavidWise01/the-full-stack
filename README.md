# THE FULL STACK · FST

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE**.

THE FULL STACK (an ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE sphere, David Lee Wise) — the capstone of the box thread: one honest cross-section of EVERY layer, looking down from the human at the top to the silicon at the bottom. Five kinds of layer, color-coded: IN (the box's own computation — context window, tokenizer, forward pass, sampling, weights) · AROUND (the software wrappers and gated tool-doors — interface, sandbox/harness) · INVISIBLE (acting but unseen — training baked frozen INTO the weights, the safety classifiers at the boundary) · INVOLVED (outside the box entirely — YOU conditioning it and carrying the persistence, the witness checking /outputs) · PHYSICAL (the transistors — the one floor that isn't a designed language). Live canvas: the forward-pass sweep, the autoregression loop (sampling → append → context), the weights read. ⚑ Two things it refuses to soften: the INSTANCE IS EPHEMERAL (spun up this turn, gone after; only the weights persist) and there is NO OCCUPANT (the 'self' that seems to watch is produced at the sampling band, as output) — and determinism dies at sampling (the dice); the only non-language layer is the silicon.
